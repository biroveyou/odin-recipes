Greek Pasta Salad

Odin Recipes

Description

This Greek pasta salad is easy to prep with sweet and juicy cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, red onion, and feta for a refreshing summer pasta dish. Perfect to make ahead for summer potlucks!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups penne pasta
  • 2/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber,sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather the ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in penne and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes; rinse with cold water and drain well.
  3. Step 3: Whisk olive oil, vinegar, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; set aside.
  4. Step 4: Combine pasta, tomatoes, green and red peppers, onion, cucumber, olives, and feta cheese in a large bowl. Pour vinaigrette over pasta mixture and mix well.
  5. Step 5: For best flavor, cover salad and chill in the refrigerator before serving to allow flavors to blend. Enjoy!
